Privacy Policy for Jammusik

Last updated: January 26, 2025

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Jammusik manages information when you use our iOS application designed for musicians to organize playlists and manage song information.

App Description

Jammusik is a tool for musicians focused on playlist organization and song management for rehearsals and live performances. Features include:

  • Playlist creation and management
  • Song tracking and marking system
  • Detailed song information storage (chords, keys, notes)
  • Music genre categorization

Information Collection

We collect minimal user data:

  • Email (for Google Sign-In authentication only)
  • Basic Google profile information

Technical Infrastructure

  • Backend: Firebase and Node.js APIs
  • Platform: iOS

Data Usage

We use collected information solely for:

  • User authentication
  • Account management
  • Service functionality

Data Storage and Security

  • Data is securely stored on Firebase
  • Industry-standard encryption protocols
  • No selling or sharing of personal data

Third-Party Services

  • Google Authentication
  • Firebase services

Your Rights

You can:

  • Access your data
  • Request data deletion
  • Modify saved information

Contact

Changes

We'll notify users of any policy updates through the app.
