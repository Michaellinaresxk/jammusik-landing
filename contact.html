Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

You can reset your password through the app's settings menu or by clicking the "Forgot Password" link on the login screen.

Can I use Jammusik offline?

Yes, many features of Jammusik work offline. However, some features like Spotify integration require an internet connection.

How do I share my playlists?

Open the playlist you want to share, tap the share icon, and choose your sharing method or directly invite other users.

Is Jammusik available in my country?

Jammusik is available worldwide. However, some features might vary depending on your region's Spotify availability.
